Welcome to Track System

Track is a web-based system designed to track student academic performance.

Enjoyable School Performance

Track makes students' experience in school much enjoyable by ensuring that a student is on the right track. Additionally, all tasks are set and organized by professor, thus needing no intervention from the student. This makes schooling easier.

Task Management

With Track, school-related tasks are highly organized and prioritized. This prevents students from forgetting an important school activity, giving him more mental space. This can result in effective time management and impressive school performance.

Performance Tracking

Using visualization such as tables and graphs, students can see from bird-view perspective their individual performance in a certain school subject. This allows students to determine beforehand whether or not a school subject being taken is passed or failed.
